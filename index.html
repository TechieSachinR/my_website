Written on a rainy evening

To you, the one reading this —

I know "sorry" can feel like just a word. Small. Overused. Not enough. So I didn't just say it — I sat down and tried to build something that might make you feel it instead.

I thought about you while the rain fell. About the way you laugh when something genuinely catches you off guard. About how you deserve someone who never makes you question whether you matter.

You matter. Endlessly.

I'm sorry for the hurt. I'm sorry for the moment I wasn't what you needed. And I'm asking — not demanding — just gently asking — for the chance to be better.

If this rain could wash anything clean, I'd ask it to wash away whatever distance sits between us right now.

— Yours, always 🌿
